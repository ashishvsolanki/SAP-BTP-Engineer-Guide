# SAP BTP Engineer Interview Preparation Guide

This repo contains a comprehensive interview preparation guide tailored for a SAP BTP Engineer role in a global manufacturing business (Reading, Berkshire; 4 days on-site; £75-85k + 15% bonus). It is designed for senior candidates (8+ years SAP, 5+ years BTP) preparing for technical and behavioral interviews.

## Structure
- tech-deep-dive.md — 22 technical deep-dive questions with expanded answers.
- scenarios.md — 12 scenario-based questions with answers.
- architecture-design.md — 9 architectural/design questions, patterns, and diagrams.
- behavioral-leadership.md — 10 behavioral/leadership STAR examples.
- company-specific.md — 6 manufacturing-focused questions.
- questions-for-interviewer.md — 10 questions to ask the interviewer.
- appendix-checklist.md — pre-interview checklist and quick reference summary.

## Quick Start
1) Start with appendix-checklist.md for a fast ramp.
2) Review tech-deep-dive.md and architecture-design.md for core technical depth.
3) Use behavioral-leadership.md to prepare STAR stories with metrics.
4) Bring manufacturing context (OTIF, MTTR, downtime, quality) into all answers.

## Notes
- Includes Mermaid diagrams for architecture and DevSecOps flows.
- Emphasizes API-first, event-driven design, security, observability, and measurable outcomes.
